# Tube Failure in HX-401 Feed Preheater

**Date:** 2024-05-20  
**Equipment:** HX-401

## Introduction
On May 20, 2024, a tube failure occurred in the HX-401 Feed Preheater, located in the Utilities Area. The failure caused a loss of heat transfer efficiency and required a shutdown for repairs.
## Incident Details
The failure was detected during a routine inspection, when a drop in heat transfer efficiency was observed. Further investigation revealed a tube rupture, likely caused by corrosion. The preheater was shut down, and the failed tube was replaced.
## Root Cause
The root cause of the failure was determined to be inadequate water treatment, leading to corrosion of the tube. The water treatment process will be reviewed and modified to prevent similar failures in the future.
