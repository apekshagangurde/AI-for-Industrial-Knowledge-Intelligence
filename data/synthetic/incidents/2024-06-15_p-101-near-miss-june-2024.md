# Minor Near-Miss: P-101 Mechanical Seal Failure

**Date:** 2024-06-15  
**Equipment:** P-101

## Introduction
A minor near-miss incident occurred on June 15, 2024, involving the P-101 Feed Pump in Process Area A. The mechanical seal failed, causing a small leak. Fortunately, the leak was detected early, and the pump was shut down before any significant damage or injury occurred.
## Incident Details
The pump was operating normally when the mechanical seal failed, causing a slow leak of process fluid. The operators noticed the leak during a routine inspection and immediately initiated shutdown procedures. The area was evacuated, and maintenance personnel were called to investigate and repair the pump.
## Root Cause
The root cause of the failure was determined to be worn-out seal faces, likely due to inadequate maintenance. The maintenance schedule for the pump will be revised to include more frequent inspections and replacements of the mechanical seal.
