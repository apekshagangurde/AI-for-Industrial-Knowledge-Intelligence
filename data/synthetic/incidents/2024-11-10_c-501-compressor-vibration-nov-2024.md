# Excessive Vibration in C-501 Instrument Air Compressor

**Date:** 2024-11-10  
**Equipment:** C-501

## Introduction
On November 10, 2024, excessive vibration was detected in the C-501 Instrument Air Compressor, located in the Utilities Area. The vibration was causing concerns about the compressor's reliability and potential downtime.
## Incident Details
The vibration was detected during a routine maintenance inspection, and the compressor was shut down for investigation. The cause of the vibration was determined to be an imbalance in the compressor's rotating assembly. The assembly was rebalanced, and the compressor was returned to service.
## Root Cause
The root cause of the vibration was determined to be inadequate maintenance, specifically a lack of regular balancing of the rotating assembly. The maintenance schedule for the compressor will be revised to include more frequent balancing and inspections.
