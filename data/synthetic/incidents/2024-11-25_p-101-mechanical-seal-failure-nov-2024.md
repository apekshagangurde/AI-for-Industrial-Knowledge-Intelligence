# Mechanical Seal Failure in P-101 Feed Pump (Recurrence)

**Date:** 2024-11-25  
**Equipment:** P-101

## Introduction
On November 25, 2024, a mechanical seal failure occurred in the P-101 Feed Pump, located in Process Area A. This incident is a recurrence of a similar failure that occurred in June 2024.
## Incident Details
The failure was more severe than the previous incident, causing a significant leak of process fluid. The pump was shut down, and the area was evacuated. Maintenance personnel were called to investigate and repair the pump.
## Root Cause
The root cause of the failure was determined to be the same as the previous incident: worn-out seal faces due to inadequate maintenance. Despite the revisions to the maintenance schedule made after the previous incident, the seal faces were not replaced as scheduled. This incident highlights the need for more rigorous adherence to the maintenance schedule and more frequent inspections of the mechanical seal.
