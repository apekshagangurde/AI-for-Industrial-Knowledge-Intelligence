# Process Area A Equipment Inspection

**Date:** 2024-01-10  
**Equipment:** P-101, P-102, V-301

### Introduction
This report outlines the findings of the routine inspection conducted in Process Area A on January 10, 2024. The inspection focused on the centrifugal pumps P-101 and P-102, as well as the feed line relief valve V-301.

### Findings
Both P-101 and P-102 were found to be in good working condition, with no significant issues noted. The mechanical seals on P-101 showed no signs of wear. V-301 was inspected and found to be functioning properly, with all settings and configurations verified as correct.

### Conclusion
The equipment in Process Area A was found to be in satisfactory condition, with no major issues requiring immediate attention. Routine maintenance tasks were performed during the inspection.
