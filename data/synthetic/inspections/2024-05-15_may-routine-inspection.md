# P-101 and HX-401 Inspection Report

**Date:** 2024-05-15  
**Equipment:** P-101, HX-401

### Introduction
This report documents the findings of the routine inspection conducted on May 15, 2024, focusing on the centrifugal pump P-101 and the feed preheater HX-401.

### Findings
P-101 was inspected, and the mechanical seals showed early signs of wear, including minor scoring on the seal faces. This is not currently affecting pump performance but should be monitored. HX-401 was found to be in good working condition, with all tubes and baffles inspected and verified as clean and free of damage.

### Conclusion
P-101's mechanical seals require monitoring due to the early signs of wear. HX-401 is in satisfactory condition. Recommendations include scheduling a follow-up inspection for P-101 within the next 6 months to assess the progression of the seal wear.
