# P-101 and P-102 Centrifugal Pumps Inspection Report

**Date:** 2024-11-05  
**Equipment:** P-101, P-102

### Introduction
This report outlines the findings of the routine inspection conducted on the centrifugal pumps P-101 and P-102 in Process Area A on November 5, 2024.

### Findings
P-101's mechanical seals, which had shown early signs of wear during the May inspection, were found to have deteriorated further. The scoring on the seal faces has increased, and there are now minor signs of leakage. P-102 was inspected and found to be in good working condition, with no significant issues noted.

### Conclusion
The mechanical seal wear on P-101 has progressed and now requires more immediate attention. It is recommended that P-101 be scheduled for maintenance to replace the mechanical seals as soon as possible to prevent further deterioration and potential failure.
