# Standard Operating Procedure for Centrifugal Pump Startup, Shutdown, and Operation

**Date:** 2023-02-15  
**Equipment:** P-101, P-102

## Introduction
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the steps for safe startup, shutdown, and operation of centrifugal pumps P-101 and P-102 in Process Area A.
## Pre-Startup Checks
* Ensure the pump and surrounding area are clear of obstacles and hazards.
* Verify the pump is properly aligned and installed.
* Check the pump and motor for any signs of damage or wear.
## Startup Procedure
1. Open the suction valve and slowly open the discharge valve.
2. Start the pump and check for any unusual vibrations or noises.
3. Gradually increase the pump speed to the desired operating range.
## Shutdown Procedure
1. Gradually decrease the pump speed to minimum.
2. Close the discharge valve and then the suction valve.
3. Stop the pump and allow it to cool down.
## Operation
* Monitor the pump performance and adjust as necessary to maintain optimal flow rates and pressure.
* Perform regular maintenance tasks, such as lubrication and filter cleaning, to ensure prolonged pump life.
## Safety Considerations
* Always follow relevant safety regulations and guidelines when working with centrifugal pumps.
* Wear personal protective equipment (PPE) when handling pump components or working near the pump.
