## Corrective Repair of Centrifugal Pump P-101
Technician Bob Johnson spent 10 hours repairing the Centrifugal Pump P-101 in Process Area A. The pump had been experiencing issues with vibration, causing a decrease in pump efficiency. The repair involved replacing the pump's bearing and balancing the impeller. The pump was then tested and found to be functioning correctly.
